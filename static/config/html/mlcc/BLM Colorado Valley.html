Description: Welcome to the BLM's Colorado River Valley Field Office (CRVFO), located in Silt, Colorado. The CRVFO administers 567,000 acres of public land in Eagle, Garfield, Pitkin, Routt, Mesa and Rio Blanco counties, Colorado. Public lands administered by the CRVFO extend from Vail in the east to Parachute in the west and from Toponas in the north to Aspen in the south.
