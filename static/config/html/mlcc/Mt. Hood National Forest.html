Protected Area: Mt. Hood National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,060,384.5
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Cascade Range Forest Reserve was established in 1893, and divided into several National Forests in 1908, when the northern portion was merged with the Bull Run Reserve (city watershed) and named Oregon National Forest. The name was changed again to Mt. Hood National Forest in 1924.
