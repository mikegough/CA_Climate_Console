Protected Area: Anaho Island National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 636.7
Gap Status: 2
Description: In 1913, President Woodrow Wilson established the ""Anaho Island Reservation"" as a preserve and breeding ground for native birds. Now known as Anaho Island National Wildlife Refuge, the island is located within Pyramid Lake, Washoe County, Nevada and is part of the Pyramid Lake Paiute Indian Reservation.
