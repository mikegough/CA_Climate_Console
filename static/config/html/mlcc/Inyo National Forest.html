Protected Area: Inyo National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 2,096,070.9
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Inyo National Forest extends 165 miles near the California and Nevada border. It covers about 2 million acres, mostly on the eastern slope of the Sierra Nevada. Elevations range from 4,000 in the Owens Valley to 14,494 at Mt. Whitney, the highest peak in the contiguous United States.
