Protected Area: Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 6,706,763.3
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest's spectacular 6.3 million acres makes it the largest national forest in the lower 48 states. Located in Nevada and a small portion of eastern California, the Forest offers year-round recreation of all types.
