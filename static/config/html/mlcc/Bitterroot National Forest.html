Protected Area: Bitterroot National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,666,102.2
Gap Status: 3
Description: Bitterroot National Forest comprises 1.587 million acres in west-central Montana and eastern Idaho, of the United States. It is located primarily in Ravalli County, Montana, but also has acreage in Idaho County, Idaho, and Missoula County, Montana.
