Protected Area: Deschutes National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,869,976.9
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Deschutes National Forest runs along Central Oregon s Cascades from the Mount Thielsen Wilderness at its southern tip to the Mount Jefferson Wilderness at the northern point. The Deschutes National Forest encompasses nearly 1.6 million acres, offering year-around recreation opportunities.
