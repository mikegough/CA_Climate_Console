Protected Area: Capitan Grande Reservation
Designation: Native American Land
Acres: 15,626.10
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: Capitan Grande is the name of the canyon through which the San Diego River once ran, about 35 miles east of San Diego. With abundant water, Indians living there had sustained themselves through farming. In 1875, a Presidential Executive Order withdrew lands from the federal domain, setting aside a number of small reservations, including the Capitan Grande Reservation from which the Viejas Band and Barona Band descended.
