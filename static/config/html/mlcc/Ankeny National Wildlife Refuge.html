Protected Area: Ankeny National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 2,816.5
Gap Status: 2
Description: The area was previously known as ""Ankeny Bottoms"" and was selected as a refuge site to provide overwintering habitat for the dusky Canada goose and other migratory waterfowl. The dusky Canada goose is a subspecies of Canada goose that was in a decline in the late 1950's and early 1960's. This was primarily due to earthquakes and floods in its nesting habitat on Alaska's Copper River Delta, and the urbanization of its wintering habitat in the Willamette Valley.
