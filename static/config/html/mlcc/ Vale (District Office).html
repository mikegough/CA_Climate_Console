Protected Area: BLM Vale (District Office)
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 5,056,099.0
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Vale District of the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) manages 5.1 million acres of public land in eastern Oregon. The district boundary runs along the Snake River between Oregon and Idaho and includes small sections of both Washington and Nevada.
