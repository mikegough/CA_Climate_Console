Protected Area: Toppenish National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 12,383.9
Gap Status: 2
Description: Thousands and thousands of ducks. That's the image first brought to mind when thinking of Toppenish National Wildlife Refuge. However, a second look reveals more there are also geese and endangered steelhead and shorebirds and upland wildlife like coyotes, mule deer and badgers and a whole host of other wildlife calling the refuge home.
