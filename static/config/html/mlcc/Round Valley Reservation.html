Protected Area: Round Valley Reservation
Designation: Native American Land
Acres: 49,389.00
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: Round Valley has been the heart of the Yuki territory ""since time began"". The Yuki have lived on their ancestral homeland (stretching from Humboldt Bay to the upper Russian River area) for over 10,000 years prior to other tribes immigrating into California. The Yuki are thought to be the original Paleo-Indians of California.
