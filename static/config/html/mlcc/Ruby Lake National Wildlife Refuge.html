Protected Area: Ruby Lake National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 40,039.8
Gap Status: 2
Description: Ruby Lake serves as a magnet for a wide diversity of wildlife species and is strategically located along migration corridors serving both the Pacific and Central Flyways. The refuge has been identified as one of 500 Globally Important Bird Areas by the American Bird Conservancy.
Link: http://www.fws.gov/refuge/ruby_lake/
