Protected Area: BLM Kanab
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 2,422,346.60
Gap Status: 3
Description: The BLM's Kanab Field Office (KFO) announced the release of the Supplemental Draft Environmental Impact Statement (SDEIS) on the Alton Coal Tract Lease By Application (LBA). The SDEIS analyzes potential impacts associated with a proposal to mine federal coal underlying nearly 3,600 acres of public and private land south of Alton, Utah.
