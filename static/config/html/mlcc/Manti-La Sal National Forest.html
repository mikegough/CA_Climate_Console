Protected Area: Manti-La Sal National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,414,061.4
Gap Status: 3
Description: The deep sandstone canyons, mountaintops, meadows, lakes, and streams of the Manti-La Sal National Forest create a diverse landscape. From the Abajos and La Sals in southeastern Utah to the Wasatch Plateau and Sanpitch mountains hundreds of miles away in central Utah, the Forest is a welcome retreat.
