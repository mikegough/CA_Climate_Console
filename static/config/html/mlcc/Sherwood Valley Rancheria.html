Protected Area: Sherwood Valley Rancheria
Designation: Native American Land
Acres: 362.5
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: Sherwood Valley Rancheria is located within our aboriginal homelands that we have used and occupied since time immemorial. Our homeland extends from approximately from the Hwy 101 corridor, through the Redwood Forests, on to the Coast. As the original stewards of this land we retain original usufructary rights to protect the land, air, water, and food sources upon our homeland. We have freely gathered coastal resources since time immemorial, and protection of the aboriginal food sources and traditional gathering places is a fundamental human right.
