Protected Area: Sequoia National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,161,660.8
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Sequoia National Forest, named for the world s largest trees, celebrates the greatest concentration of giant sequoia groves in the world. Protected within the Giant Sequoia National Monument, these groves and the areas around them are managed by the U.S. Forest Service for today and for future generations.
