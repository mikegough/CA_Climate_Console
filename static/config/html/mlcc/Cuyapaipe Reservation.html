Protected Area: Cuyapaipe Reservation
Designation: Native American Land
Acres: 5,101.20
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: The Cuyapaipe Reservation was established on February 10, 1891, following the executive order of January 12, 1891. The Cuyapaipe Reservation is located 10 miles north of Interstate 8, and 68 miles east of San Diego in Pine Valley and the Laguna Mountains in the southeastern part of San Diego county.
