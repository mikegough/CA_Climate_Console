Protected Area: BLM Kremmling
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 378,123.00
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Kremmling Field Office manages 377,860 acres of land within a boundary four times the size of the State of Rhode Island! If a box was drawn around this piece of BLM, it would extend approximately east to the Continental Divide, west to Steamboat Springs and Vail, south to Interstate-70, and north to the Wyoming border. The landscape is varied between high mountain peaks and open sage-brush parks. Our visitors regularly enjoy our vast array of recreation opportunities including rafting, hunting, and off-road use.
