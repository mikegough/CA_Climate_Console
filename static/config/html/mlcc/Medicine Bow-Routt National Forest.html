Protected Area: Medicine Bow-Routt National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 4,592,726.0
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Medicine Bow-Routt National Forests (almost 2.9 million acres) extend from north central Colorado to central Wyoming. The national forests encompass portions of many mountain ranges, including Gore Range, Flat Tops, Parks Range, Elkhead, Medicine Bow Mountains, Sierra Madre, and Laramie Range.
