Protected Area: BLM Coos Bay (District Office)
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 325,873.70
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Coos Bay District of the U.S. Bureau of Land Management (BLM) manages 325,000 acres of public lands in the Coast Range of southwestern Oregon. BLM is committed to a multiple-use approach to managing your public lands. Major programs include forestry, recreation, and fish and wildlife habitat management.
