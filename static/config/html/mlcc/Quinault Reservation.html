Protected Area: Quinault Reservation
Designation: Tribal Lands
Acres: 207,411.0
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: We are among the small number of Americans who can walk the same beaches, paddle the same waters, and hunt the same lands our ancestors did centuries ago. The Quinault Indian Nation (QIN) consists of the Quinault and Queets tribes and descendants of five other coastal tribes: Quileute, Hoh, Chehalis, Chinook, and Cowlitz .
