Protected Area: Cokeville Meadows National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 27,585.2
Gap Status: 2
Description: Cokeville Meadows National Wildlife Refuge(CMNWR) south of Cokeville, Wyoming is centered around a 20-mile stretch of the Bear River and its associated wetlands and uplands. The Refuge was established in 1992. While the approved acquisition boundary for the Refuge totals 26,657 acres, only 9,259 have been purchased or are protected through conservation easements to date. Land acquisition is ongoing from willing sellers only.
