Protected Area: Tahoe National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 1,179,785.0
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Tahoe National Forest is located in the northern Sierra Nevada (east of Sacramento) and extends from the foothills across the Sierra crest to the California state line. It includes over 850,000 acres of public land interspersed with 350,000 acres of private land in a checker board ownership pattern.
