Protected Area: Kootenai National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 2,624,577.4
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Kootenai National Forest is located in the Northwest corner of Montana and the Northeast corner of Idaho on the Canadian border. Providing abundant recreation and a wealth of natural resources, the Kootenai is a perfect place to relax and enjoy your National Forests!
