Protected Area: BLM Worland
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 2,177,430.60
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Worland Field Office manages more than two million acres of public lands in the southern half of the Bighorn Basin. Located in northcentral Wyoming, these lands provide a variety of resources and uses including wild horses, wildlife habitat, scenic vistas, recreation opportunities, mineral development and livestock grazing.
