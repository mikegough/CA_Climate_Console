Protected Area: Caribou-Targhee National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 3,077,295.4
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Caribou-Targhee National Forest occupies over 3 million acres and stretches across southeastern Idaho, from the Montana, Utah, and Wyoming borders. This Forest is also home to the Curlew National Grassland.
