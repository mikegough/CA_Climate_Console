Protected Area: BLM Arcata
Designation: BLM Lands
Acres: 220,672.20
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Arcata Field Office is responsible for the administration of natural resources, lands, and mineral programs on over 200,000 acres of public land in Northwestern California. The Area includes the King Range National Conservation Area, Headwaters Forest Reserve and the Trinidad Gateway to the California Coastal National Monument.
