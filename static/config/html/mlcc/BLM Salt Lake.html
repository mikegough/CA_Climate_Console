Designation: BLM Lands

Description: The Salt Lake Field Office is located in the northwest corner of Utah and encompasses 3.3 million acres of public lands. Much of this area is part of the Great Basin region, a place of isolated mountain ranges separated by desert playas and wide sweeping sagebrush flats.
