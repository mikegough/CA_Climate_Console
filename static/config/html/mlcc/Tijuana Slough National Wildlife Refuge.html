Protected Area: Tijuana Slough National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 1,460.5
Gap Status: 2
Description: This Refuge is also designated as a Wetland of International Importance by the Ramsar Wetlands Convention. The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) administers the reserve system and supports research and education activities along with our partners at California State Parks and the Southwest Wetlands Interpretive Association (SWIA).
Link: http://www.fws.gov/refuge/tijuana_slough/
