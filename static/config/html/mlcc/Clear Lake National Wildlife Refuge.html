Protected Area: Clear Lake National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 33,578.7
Gap Status: 2
Description: Clear Lake Refuge in northeastern California consists of approximately 20,000 acres of open water surrounded by over 26,000 acres of upland bunchgrass, low sagebrush, and juniper habitat. Small, rocky islands in the lake provide nesting sites for American white pelicans, double-crested cormorants, and other colonial nesting birds.
