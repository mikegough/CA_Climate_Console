Protected Area: Modoc National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 8,192.50
Gap Status: 2
Description: The 7,021-acre Modoc Refuge is located along the South Fork of the Pit River in Modoc County, southeast of the town of Alturas, in extreme northeastern California (Figure 1). The Refuge is located on the western edge of the Great Basin, a high elevation, cold desert environment. The Refuge consists of wetland, reservoir, riparian, sagebrush-steppe, and cropland habitats.
