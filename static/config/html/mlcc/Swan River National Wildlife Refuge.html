Protected Area: Swan River National Wildlife Refuge
Designation: National Wildlife Refuge
Acres: 2,642.0
Gap Status: 2
Description: Swan River National Wildlife Refuge (NWR) was acquired by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service in 1973 to be managed for the benefit of migratory birds. This 1,568 acre Refuge is part of the Benton Lake National Wildlife Refuge Complex and is administered by the Refuge staff at Benton Lake NWR located just north of Great Falls, Montana.
