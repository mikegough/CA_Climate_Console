Protected Area: Wind River Indian Reservation
Designation: Native American Land
Acres: 1,923,760.70
Gap Status: Unknown
Description: Wyoming's Wind River Country is home to the seventh largest Indian reservation in the country. Encompassing more than 2.2 million acres, the Wind River Indian Reservation is home to the Eastern Shoshone and the Northern Arapaho tribes. Visitors to the area can go to powwows and museums to learn more about the local culture, or can recreate on thousands of acres of vast, unspoiled reservation lands with special permits.
