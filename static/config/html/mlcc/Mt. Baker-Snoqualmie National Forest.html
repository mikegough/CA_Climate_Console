Protected Area: Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest
Designation: National Forest
Acres: 2,025,243.8
Gap Status: 3
Description: The Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest, one of the most visited forests in the country, located on the western slopes of the Cascade mountain range extending over 140 miles between Seattle, Washington and Vancouver, British Columbia. Here you will find glacier-covered peaks, spectacular mountain meadows and old-growth forests.
